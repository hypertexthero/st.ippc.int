Heading Level One
=================

A paragraph with *emphasized*, **bold** text and 
[a link](http://www.ippc.int) to a webpage. You can also 
do reference-style [links][markdowndocs], with the reference 
in the bottom of the page.

- List item
- List item
    - Nested list item indented 4 spaces
        - Nested list item indented 8 spaces

-   Another list item, but this one has...

    Multiple paragraphs!

-   Yet another list item.

A Second-Level Heading
----------------------

1. Ordered List item
2. Ordered List item
    1. Nested ordered list item indented 4 spaces
    2. List style is controlled by the stylesheet (numbers, letters, etc)
        3. Indented by 8 spaces
3. Ordered List item

> A blockquotation, said by someone important.
    >> Nested blockquotation

Horizontal Rulers
-----------------

Three or more asterisks:

* * *

Line Breaks
-----------

End a line with two or more spaces:

Roses are red,  
Violets are blue.

Footnotes
---------

This footnote will appear at the bottom of the document[^1].

The footnote doesn't have to be a number[^nonumber].

[^1]: Told you it'd be here at the bottom.
[^nonumber]: See, not a number.
